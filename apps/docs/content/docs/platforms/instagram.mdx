Instagram share links (the short redirect URLs the app generates) are automatically resolved to their canonical URL before fetching.

## Supported URLs

```
https://instagram.com/p/<shortcode>
https://instagram.com/reel/<shortcode>
https://instagram.com/reels/<shortcode>
https://instagram.com/stories/<user>/<shortcode>
https://instagram.com/share/<shortcode>
```

## Supported features

### Text

The post caption.

### Media

Up to 10 items — single images, carousels, videos, and reels.

### Stats

Comments and likes.
